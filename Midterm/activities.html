Top Activities

Hike Mount Pelée

Ascend the dramatic volcano for panoramic views and unique volcanic geology. Guides are available for safety and local context. Best in the dry season for visibility and trail conditions.

Snorkel & Dive the Coral Reefs

Martinique has healthy reef systems ideal for snorkeling and SCUBA diving. Expect abundant reef fish, sea turtles, and clear waters—several operators offer guided excursions.

Explore Fort-de-France Markets

Visit local markets for spices, rum, and textile crafts. Engaging with local vendors provides cultural insight and supports small businesses—an ethical tourism practice.
