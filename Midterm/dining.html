Recommended Restaurants

Le Jardin Créole

A family-run restaurant serving traditional Creole seafood and seasonal produce. Noted for its rum-infused desserts and warm hospitality. Reservations recommended on weekends.

XO Bistro

Modern bistro with ocean views that blends French technique with local ingredients—think grilled snapper with tropical fruit chutney and fine local rums.

Rue Market Flavors

Street vendors and market stalls offering accras (cod fritters), boudin créole, and fresh fruit juices. A lively, authentic experience best enjoyed mid-day.
