Recommended Restaurants

La Mandoline

A family-run restaurant serving traditional Creole seafood and seasonal produce. Noted for its rum-infused desserts and warm hospitality.

XO Bistro

Modern bistro with ocean views that blends French technique with local ingredients—think grilled snapper with tropical fruit chutney.

Anba Fèy Tôl La

Affordable, delicious menu with quick, friendly service. Unique decor and a beautiful coastline view.
