Shadowstrike Dodgeball Squad

Welcome to the Shadowstrike Squad — a dodgeball team built on precision, style, and pure power. These four elite characters bring unmatched agility, intelligence, and supernatural strength to every match.

With her acrobatic skills and Witch Time reflexes, Bayonetta can dodge anything and return every throw with lethal elegance.

Sephiroth

Hela

Seto Kaiba

Kaiba’s sharp intellect, flawless strategy, and iron determination make him the team's mastermind and tactical backbone.

Shadow the Hedgehog

With Chaos Control, unmatched speed, and razor-sharp focus, Shadow can warp across the court, dodge any throw, and strike back before opponents even react.
