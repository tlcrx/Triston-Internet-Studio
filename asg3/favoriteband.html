Aya Nakamura

Bio: Aya Coco Danioko (born 10 May 1995), known professionally as Aya Nakamura, is a French-Malian pop singer. She rose to fame in 2018 with her hit single "Djadja" which topped charts across Europe and beyond. With her blend of Afrobeat, R&B, and pop, Nakamura has become one of the most influential voices in contemporary French music.

Aya Nakamura is my favorite artist because her music combines catchy rhythms with a unique cultural influence. Her songs are both modern and global, bringing together different styles in a way that feels fresh and inspiring.
