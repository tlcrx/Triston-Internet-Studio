My Least Favorite Artist

Taylor Swift

Before anything else, I want to emphasize that I do not consider Taylor Swift to be a horrible artist. She is extremely talented and has had a massive influence on music and culture. However, I personally have not found many of her songs that I connect with. An exception would be "...Ready For It?" from the Reputation album, which I genuinely enjoy.

This might be because my favorite genre of music is R&B/Pop, and Taylor’s sound doesn’t often feature R&B-inspired elements. Because of this, I naturally gravitate more toward artists whose music leans into that style.
